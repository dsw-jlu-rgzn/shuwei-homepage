About

I am Shuwei Dong. I completed both my undergraduate and master's training at Jilin University, then spent two years at Momenta working on practical AI systems.

My current work focuses on medical large language models, especially clinical reasoning over longitudinal electronic health records, dependable evaluation, and systems that can support high-stakes healthcare use cases.

Research

I am interested in building medical AI systems that connect strong model capability with verifiable clinical evidence and reproducible evaluation.

  • Medical large language models

    Clinical-domain LLMs for reasoning, generation, and decision support.

  • Longitudinal EHR reasoning

    Methods that use patient history, temporal context, and structured clinical evidence.

  • Reliable evaluation

    Benchmarks and analysis workflows for trustworthy medical AI behavior.
